About this library

The rule

A practice belongs here if it leaves you feeling strong, regulated, clear, grounded, creative, or gently restored.

It doesn't belong if it leaves you feeling compared, behind, not enough, rushed, or performative.

What makes a good practice

1
Clear alignment cues Joints, pelvis, spine position — with transitions guided, not rushed.
2
Modifications offered Easier and stronger options, props acknowledged, rest is never shamed.
3
Breath is central Inhale/exhale cued throughout, not just mentioned at the start. Pace serves the breath.
4
Mindfulness woven in Grounding, presence, self-trust — lightly, without preaching.
5
Teacher energy over production value Practice over performance. No "yoga model" aesthetic-first energy.
