
# 🌙 Cycle-Based Yoga Video Selection Guide

## Your Core Standards (Non-Negotiables)

When evaluating a video, ask:

### 1️⃣ Alignment Cues
A good video should:
- Cue joints clearly (stack shoulders over wrists, knees over ankles)
- Mention pelvis position / rib cage / spine orientation
- Offer setup before movement
- Give transition guidance (not just jump from pose to pose)

🚩 Red flag:
- “Flow with me” style with no technical guidance
- Mostly music, minimal instruction
- Rapid transitions without setup

---

### 2️⃣ Modifications & Accessibility
A good teacher:
- Offers easier + stronger variations
- Mentions props (blocks, blankets)
- Gives permission language (“you can stay here”)
- Does not shame rest

🚩 Red flag:
- Only advanced expressions shown
- No modifications offered
- “Push through” culture

---

### 3️⃣ Breath Is Central
For you, this is huge.

Look for:
- Inhale/exhale cued intentionally
- Breath pacing during holds
- Mentions of nervous system regulation
- Occasional pranayama emphasis

🚩 Red flag:
- Breath only mentioned at beginning
- Fast pace that overrides breath
- No integration of breath + movement

---

### 4️⃣ Integration of Yoga Philosophy (Lightly Woven)
You like when teachers:
- Weave in mindfulness naturally
- Reference grounding, presence, self-trust
- Integrate subtle limb teachings without preaching

🚩 Red flag:
- Pure aesthetics / body-focused commentary
- Spiritual bypass language
- “Manifest abs” energy

---

### 5️⃣ Not Aesthetic-First
Avoid:
- Excessively posed thumbnail culture
- Overly cinematic, under-instructed videos
- “Yoga model” vibe

You want:
- Teacher energy > production value
- Practice > performance

# 🌿 Phase-Based Selection Criteria

When adding new videos, match them to phase energy.

---

## 🩸 Menstrual (Bleeding)
Look for:
- Restorative
- Yin
- Hip + low back release
- Nervous system downshift
- Seated or supine majority

Avoid:
- Core-heavy
- Inversions
- Fast sun salutations

---

## 🌱 Early Follicular
Look for:
- Gentle mobility
- Slow flow
- Rebuilding energy
- 15–20 min

Avoid:
- HIIT-style vinyasa

---

## 🌼 Mid Follicular
Look for:
- Expansion
- Openers
- Moderate vinyasa
- Balanced strength

---

## 🌕 Ovulation
Look for:
- Strong flows
- Power yoga
- Playful balance work
- 10–20 min high energy

Avoid:
- Overly restorative

---

## 🌾 Early Luteal
Look for:
- Steady strength
- Controlled pace
- Longer holds
- Breath-guided grounding

---

## 🌙 Mid Luteal
Look for:
- Hips
- Lower back
- Gentle detox flows
- Emotional regulation themes

---

## 🌑 Late Luteal (Creative + Inward)
Look for:
- Yin
- Slow transitions
- Breathwork
- Reflective tone
- Creative metaphors

Avoid:
- Intense heat
- Competitive vibe

---

# 📋 Quick Add Checklist (Save This)

When evaluating a new video, score it 1–5 on:

- Alignment cues
- Modifications offered
- Breath integration
- Nervous-system awareness
- Tone (supportive vs aesthetic-driven)

If average score < 3.5 → Don’t add it.

---

# 📌 Video Length Rule

Your ideal window: 10–25 minutes.

- Under 10 → must be breath-focused or power burst
- Over 25 → only if phase-appropriate (yin/restorative)

---

# 🌸 Spanish Integration Strategy

For Spanish videos:
- Prioritize clear verbal cueing
- Avoid overly fast speakers
- Good for follicular + ovulatory phases (learning energy higher)
- Great for breath videos mid/late luteal
